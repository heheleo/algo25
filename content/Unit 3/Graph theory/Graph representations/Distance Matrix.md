A $|N|\times|N|$ matrix that stores the total distance taken to travel from one node to the other. Stores distance not weight. If unreachable, distance is infinity.
## Notation
Distance matrix is commonly notated as $D$.

| c   | 7   | 7   | 0   | 1   |
| d   | 6   | 16  | 9   | 0   |
